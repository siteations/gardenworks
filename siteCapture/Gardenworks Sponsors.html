Meet Our Sponsors

The GardenWorks Project offers corporate sponsorship opportunities at events throughout the year. Corporate volunteer days are also offered seasonally, and these service opportunities are hands-on, eye-opening, and best of all, does good for our neighbors in need! Please consider becoming a corporate sponsor this year. For more information, please contact us at info@gardenworksproject.org.

Wish List

All of our families receive a set of tools to help ensure their gardening success. Please contact us if you have any of the following items in new or very good condition that you would like to donate.

Desired Tools in Good Condition:
  • Garden tools including shovels, rakes, hoes, broadforks, trowels, hand-cultivators
  • Tomato cages
  • Lead-free hoses
  • Hose nozzles
  • Instructional vegetable gardening
    books and magazines
    (we would be especially grateful for gardening books for kids and adults in Spanish)

Start too many seedlings this spring? We would be happy to share them with our clients.

If you are interested in making a donation of any of these items, or have something else that you think we could make good use of, please contact us.
